About Me

I'm Atal Agarwal, the founder of Legal Bridge and Open Sphere, where we help immigrants navigate the complex world of visas and immigration services. But beyond business, I'm passionate about cultivating great energy through the harmony of mind, body, and soul.

My journey is an inside-out approach to life, drawing wisdom from Buddhism, Stoicism, and countless readings on spiritual growth. I believe that when our mind, body, and soul rhythm together, great energy is created - and this energy can be experienced by anyone in this world.

"Atal Loves You" means loving yourself first because self-love is truly the gateway to loving others. Loving the world is simply loving yourself - just the way you are. An imperfect human being looking to grow and create a better version of yourself.
